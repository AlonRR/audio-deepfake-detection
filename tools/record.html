Before you start: quiet room, no AC or fan. Mic about 20 cm away, and keep that distance steady. Watch the level bar below — aim for it bouncing in the green; if it hits red you are clipping, so back off a little.
  • Mistakes are fine. Pause a beat, re-say the sentence, carry on. Bad takes get dropped when the audio is segmented.
  • Read as if telling a story to a friend — natural pace, let your voice move. Flat reading makes a flat clone.
  • Browser noise-suppression, echo-cancellation and auto-gain are switched off deliberately — they smear the voice detail the model needs.
